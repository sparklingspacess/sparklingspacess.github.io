Red & Dead

This mod is not complete, this is an early version.
Cinematic overhaul of the Euphoria in Red Dead Redemption 2.
You need the RDR2 ScriptHook and Lenny's Mod Loader for this to work, once you have installed those you will need to download this mod, extract its contents and put "redndead" in the "lml" folder in your games directory.
